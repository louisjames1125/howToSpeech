Setup: Stance

  • Keep Your Feet Shoulder Width Apart
  • Aim with Your Shoulders and Hips
  • Slightly Flex Your Knees
  • Maintain a Good Posture
  • 50/50 Weight Between Toes and Heels
Back Next

Setup: Placement

  • Keep Your Shoulders Square
  • Grip with Your Fingers and NOT the Palm of Your Hand
  • Grab the Club Starting with Your Target Arm
  • Keep Your Head in the Same Position
  • Focus Your Eyes on the Back of the Ball

Backswing

  • Keep Your Shoulders Beneath the Chin
  • DON'T Break the Wrists
  • Maintain a Straight Target Arm
  • Keep Your Head Square
  • DON'T Drag the Club

Follow-through

  • Shift Your Weight Forward
  • Rotate Your Hips
  • Maintain a Straight Target Arm
  • Turn Your Hands Over
  • DON'T Pull Your Head Up
